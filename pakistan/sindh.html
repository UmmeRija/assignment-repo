Sindh

Sindh, Pakistan's second-largest province, boasts a rich cultural heritage and diverse geography. Its capital, Karachi, is the largest city. Sindh's economy thrives on agriculture and industry, with natural resources like coal, gas, and oil. Historical sites like Mohenjo-Daro and Thatta showcase its ancient Indus Valley Civilization roots. The province is known for its vibrant music, Sufi traditions, and delicious cuisine. From deserts to national parks, Sindh's varied landscape offers a unique experience.
