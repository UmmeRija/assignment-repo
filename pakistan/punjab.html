punjab

Punjab, Pakistan's largest province, is a land of rich cultural heritage and diverse landscapes. Located in the eastern part of the country, Punjab is bordered by India, Sindh, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, and Balochistan. The province is home to over 110 million people, with Lahore as its capital and largest city. Punjab's fertile soil and abundant water resources make it the country's agricultural hub, producing wheat, rice, sugarcane, and cotton. The province boasts a storied history, with Mughal-era monuments like the Lahore Fort and Badshahi Mosque standing testament to its glorious past. Punjab's vibrant culture is reflected in its traditional cuisine, folk music, and colorful festivals like the Basant Kite Festival. The province is also home to several prestigious educational institutions, including the University of the Punjab and Lahore University of Management Sciences. With its blend of history, culture, and natural beauty, Punjab is a treasure trove waiting to be explored.
